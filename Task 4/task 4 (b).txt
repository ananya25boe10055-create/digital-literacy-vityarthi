Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist for College Students


✔️Do’s
✔️ Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
✔️ Maintain privacy settings to protect personal information
✔️ Share accurate and verified information only
✔️ Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development
✔️ Build a positive and professional online presence
✔️ Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy discussions  


❌ Don’ts
❌ Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)
❌ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
❌ Do not spread fake news or unverified information
❌ Avoid cyberbullying or trolling others
❌ Do not overshare your personal life publicly
❌ Avoid wasting excessive time that affects studies or productivity
